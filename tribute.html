Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of India’s greatest scientists and the 11th President of India. He inspired millions with his simplicity, vision, and dedication to the nation.

Known as the “Missile Man of India”, he played a key role in India’s space and defense programs. His life teaches us the power of dreams, hard work, and humility.

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
